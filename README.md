```bash

git clone https://github.com/Cdg192/throughthetreess.git
cd throughthetreess
```

## About the Game

Join Caleb and his sidekick Chazz on an epic quest to rescue Ptouneigh from the clutches of the evil sorceress Tracey. Armed with a Gibson Guitar and unwavering determination, battle through cultists and face off against the Sionist conspiracy.

### Features (Python Version)
- 🎨 2D pixel art graphics with character animations
- 📐 Top-down exploration view
- ⚔️ Turn-based combat system with multiple enemies
- 🎬 Combat animations and effects
- 🎵 Gibson Guitar-powered attacks with fun messages
- 📊 Real-time battle log
- 🏰 5 unique locations to explore
- 👿 Multiple enemy types with increasing difficulty
- 🏆 Boss battle with Tracey the Sorceress

### Controls (Python Game)
- **SPACE** - Start Battle / Attack in combat
- **D** - Defend in combat
- **R** - Run from combat / Restart game
- **E** - Explore next location
- **A** - Search area
- **C** - Check status

### Story
Your beloved cat **Ptouneigh** has been captured by the evil sorceress **Tracey** and her Sionist cult! You must journey through dangerous locations, defeat cultists and guardians, and face Tracey herself in her dark tower. With your Gibson Guitar as your weapon and Chazz as your loyal companion, can you rescue your furry friend?

### Characters
- **Caleb** - Your hero, armed with a Gibson Guitar
- **Ptouneigh** - Your beloved cat (to be rescued!)
- **Chazz** - Your loyal sidekick and party member
- **Tracey** - The evil sorceress with poor vision
- **The Sionist Cult** - Religious fanatics serving Tracey

### Locations
1. **Cottage** - Your home, where the adventure begins
2. **Village** - A peaceful town with Sionist symbols
3. **Dark Forest** - Home to corrupted creatures
4. **Sionist Temple** - Cult headquarters with dark magic
5. **Dark Tower** - Tracey's lair at the top of the world

Enjoy the adventure! 🎮
